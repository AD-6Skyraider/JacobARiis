Jake's Place for Help and Healing

Resources in Bowling Green and Warren Co. KY for those in or facing homelessness and / or needing domestic abuse support as well as other aid and resources.

Our Mission Statement:
"Jacob A. Riis" is a resource page for the homeless and those seeking resources for domestic abuse in Bowling Green and Warren Co Kentucky. As our tagline states, "Jake's Place: Help for Healing," our mission is to create and bring to the people a simple and unfettered resource for those experiencing homelessness as well as those seeking help to escape domestic abuse. other resources.
