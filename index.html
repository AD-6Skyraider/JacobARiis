Jacob A. Riis: Jake's Place for Help and Healing

Resources in Bowling Green and Warren Co. KY for those in or facing homelessness (and other aid).

Our Mission Statement:
"Jacob A. Riis" is a resource page for the homeless and those seeking resources for domestic abuse in Bowling Green and Warren Co Kentucky. As our tagline states, "Jake's Place: Help for Healing," our mission is to create and bring to the people a simple and unfettered resource for those experiencing homelessness as well as those seeking help to escape domestic abuse. Our goal is to provide contact information on shelters, food pantries, and domestic violence resources. In addition, we plan to provide other resources, for mental health, medical health, drug & alcohol support/recovery, suicide prevention, and other resources.
